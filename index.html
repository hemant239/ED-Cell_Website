Entrepreneurship Development Cell MNIT Jaipur

ED Cell has been set up in the Deptment of Management Studies, Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur. The Cell is sponsoured by Department of Science and Technology, Government of India to act as an institutional mechanism for providing various services including information to budiing S&T entrepreneur. Objective of ED Cell is to motivate students and provide necessary guidance and assitance to become sucessful entrepreneur.

Flagship Events

Aspire B-Quiz 2k18

The fourth season of the biggest intra Bquiz, Aspire 2K18 on 4th February 2018, organised by the ED Cell. With a mass participation of students, the prelims kicked off with the auditorium enveloped in positive vibes. A total of six teams made it to the final round. The final round of the B-Quiz kept not only the audience at the edge of their seats but even got the participants racking their brains as the quizmaster threw questions of all fields at them. All in all, this b- quiz didn't just witness a thrilling competition but also an enthusiastic participation.

Startup Nivesh

Startup निवेश is one of the biggest flagship events of ED Cell that is held during Blitzschlag.It gives a live experience of Dalal Street which offers participants the chance to showcase their wit and test their business acumen. It presents the opportunity to awaken the investor in you and shows a glimpse of the enthralling world of real life market and risks.
The fourth season of this event marked the introduction of Crypto Currency and had everyone at the edge of their seats as the participants had to navigate through all types of ups and downs in the market.
